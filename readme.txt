== Installation ==
= Stylish Internal Links =
1. Go to Plugins > Add New Plugin*, search for **Stylish Internal Links** and click "*Install now*"
2. Alternative method, download the plugin and Upload the Stylish Internal Links folder to the /wp-content/plugins/directory
3. Activate the plugin
4. Now Go to Plugin settings page under WordPress Settings page.
5. And Choose your Favourite color for the Stylish Internal links
6. That's all

OR, See detailed doc, [how to install a WordPress plugin](https://codex.wordpress.org/Managing_Plugins#Installing_Plugins).

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Stylish Internal Links? =
Internal linking has been a crucial aspect of the seo. Although people often leave about the internal linking strategies but it’s the main seo factor in 2016.
Normal interlinks aren’t that great to attract your visitors eyeballs. So to improve your internal linking click through rate and attract your visitors we are have launched the stylish internal links to embrace your visitor’s engagement.


= Why to use this plugin? =

Stylish internal links is used to increase your blogs engagement. If you want to display the normal internal links then your visitor might not grab it.
But when you add more color and stylish pattern in your post then visitors tends to click that link because of the curiosity.
This plugin helps you to display stylish internal links on your WordPress post.


= Any Benifits? =

Yes, you are having some great benefits by using this plugin.

= Does this plugin support all themes? =

Yes, absolutely it support all the WordPress themes. If you didn't work on your theme just inform us. We'll help to resolve it the next version and we'll update it.

= Does it affect your plugin and themes? =

No, it doesn't affect your plugin and themes. It a very light weighted plugin and doesn't affect other files and codes on your site.  So you don't have to worry about your themes or plugins after installing this plugin.

I am constantly improving this plugin. So you won't find much errors. If you want to file a support ticket then just create a support ticket at WordPress forum and I will work on that issues as fast as I can and get the better results for you.
